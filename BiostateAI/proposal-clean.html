This 4-week framework can be adjusted based on your feedback and evolving requirements. We'll work together to ensure each milestone aligns with your vision.

Week 1: Foundation & Core Setup

Getting the fundamentals right

Establish React/Next.js architecture, implement the landing page with basic chat interface, and create the responsive design foundation.

Week 2: Advanced Chat & User Experience

Building the intelligent conversation system
